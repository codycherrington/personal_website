About Me

My name is Cody Cherrington. I am an Applied Maths and Computer Science student at Texas State University, set to graduate in Spring 2024. My career interests include developing applications to solve mathematical/computational problems, data analysis, software engineering, or motorsport engineering. Outside of my professional life, I enjoy watching motorsports, specifically F1 and Indycar. I also enjoy fishing, hiking, running, and playing ultimate frisbee.

Early life

I was born in a small town in Russia called Nizhny Novgorod on November 22, 1998. Six months later, I was adopted and brought over to Texas. I grew up in a small town south of Austin, Texas called Wimberley. During that time, I played many sports for Wimberley High School such as football, basketball, track and field, and powerlifting. After I graduated High School, I studied Kinesiology at The University of North Texas in Denton, Texas. My time as a North Texas student was short lived, as after one semester, I transferred to Texas State University. Once I transferred to Texas State, I switched my major to Applied Math, and have been working on that degree ever since.
